I am a Professional Photographer with nearly 28 years experience taking pictures in the Bakersfield area. I attended California State University Bakersfield (CSUB) and Brooks Institute of Photography in Santa Barbara, CA. I worked for 21 years with The Bakersfield Californian and currently freelance for Bakersfield Life Magazine. I also own Greg Nichols Photography.

Through my work I've been lucky to meet a lot of great people and I've experienced some really terrific places. Bakersfield is a wonderful town and it has a lot to offer. Documenting its diversity has been a privilege and very rewarding experience. I know the best is yet to come.

~ Greg
